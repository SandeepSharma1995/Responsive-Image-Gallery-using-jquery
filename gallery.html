357.367 k.m Between Kasol and New Delhi


Kasol is a village in Himachal Pradesh, northern India. It is situated in Parvati Valley, on the banks of Parvati river, on the way between Bhuntar to Manikaran. Kasol has recently transformed into a hub for backpackers, who are attracted to the scenic valley, untouched hills, low population, and good climate throughout the year. Kasol is also a base for Himalayan trekking, to SarPass, Yanker Pass, Pin Parbati Pass and Khiriganga.
